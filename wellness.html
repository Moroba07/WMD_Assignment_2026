Wellness

Stay calm, focused, and ready to learn.

Use breathing, sleep, and burnout tips to protect your energy before, during, and after study sessions.

🫁

Box breathing

Inhale, hold, exhale, hold. Repeat for one minute before you start a hard topic.

🌙

Sleep first

Set a bedtime alarm so revision does not steal from rest and memory.

💧

Hydrate often

Keep water nearby to avoid the tired, foggy feeling that kills concentration.
